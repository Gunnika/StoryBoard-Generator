A Crab one day grew disgusted with the sands in which he lived.He decided to take a stroll to the meadow not far inland. Therehe would find better fare than briny water and sand mites. So offhe crawled to the meadow. But there a hungry Fox spied him, andin a twinkling, ate him up, both shell and claw.
